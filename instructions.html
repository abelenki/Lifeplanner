Instructions

To add a message to your to do list, budget or flashcards, just hover over the New Post button, type a message following the official Life Planner format, check off your selection(s), and press submit! For the formats, please see below.

Flashcards Format:

Question:Answer

Budget Format:

Don't let this be your piggy bank.

The Life Planner will read your message and look for + or - signs followed by a number. This will be added to your budget, and everything else will be ignored. Example: I lost -20 dollars today. :(

To Do List instructions:

Simply write the time as hour:minute anywhere in your message and the Life Planner will take care of everything!

FavSites instructions:

ex. @google.ca You do not need to select a place for it to display.
